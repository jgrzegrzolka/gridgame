How to play

Each cell sits on a row and a column. Pick a country whose flag matches both. For example, an "Asia" row and a "red" column needs a flag that is both Asian and red. First to line up three wins.

Rows and columns normally ask about the flag itself and the continent. Advanced mode also asks about country data, like population, GDP or forest cover.
